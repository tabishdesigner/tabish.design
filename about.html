About me
Crafting Engaging Experiences through UI/UX and Graphic Design
Hey there, I’m Tabish

I am a UI/UX and Graphic Designer with over 7 years of experience creating intuitive, user-centered digital experiences and visually compelling designs. I specialize in branding, web apps, mobile experiences, and graphic design, combining creativity with strategy to deliver solutions that are both functional and aesthetically engaging.

I thrive in collaborative environments, using wireframing, prototyping, visual storytelling, and design systems to help businesses enhance user engagement and achieve results. With a strong background in both digital and graphic design, I craft experiences that are not just seen, but genuinely felt.
